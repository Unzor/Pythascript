Pythascript

Pythascript puts Python inside your browser! Here's the demo for Pythascript:

This gets the code of your .py file, compiles it to Python, then puts it inside a textbox.

demo.py code:

 print("This message is made by Pythascript.")

print("Good luck on your first voyage!")
